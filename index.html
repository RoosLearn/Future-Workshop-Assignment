Carbon Footprint Labels

Simple labels to explain CO₂ impact like nutrition facts on food!

Example:

Product A: Saves 1.2 kg CO₂ → That's like 2 hours of fresh air in a forest!

Product B: Emits 5.6 kg CO₂ → Equal to driving 25 km by car.

What the Label Shows

It can show the impact as: Breathable air saved, amount of trees planted, equivalent car mileage reduced or distance covered.

Why This Works

Ordinary people relate better to real-life effects than technical numbers.

Air Credits

A reward system for choosing recycled construction materials

How It Works

Use at least one recycled material in a construction project → earn 1 Air Credit.

What You Get

Credits can give discounts on eco-products, services, or community recognition. It can even be rewarded as monetary benefits. It can be first implemented in municipalities or even shops.

Why It Matters

Build awareness through action. Show people that sustainable choices are rewarding and visible.
